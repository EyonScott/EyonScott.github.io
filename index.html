About Me

I'm an Oakland-based freelancer passionate about the intersection of multimedia and social impact. Prior to exploring the world of web development, I got my feet wet in UC Berkeley's Film & Media Studies department. I now aim to showcase the beauty & voices of local communities through visual storytelling.

Between projects, I can be found at art & music events, the occasional "FanCon," or exploring my lovely hometown.
