About Me

From an early age, I've been invested in art and multimedia. With a background in Film and Media Studies at UC Berkeley, I now aim to translate my ideas through various forms of visual storytelling.

Along with freelancing, I'm an archival associate at the Kapor Center for Social Impact. I'm also currently learning front-end web development at Hackbright Academy, the engineering school for women.
