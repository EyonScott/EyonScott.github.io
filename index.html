About Me

I'm an Oakland-based freelancer passionate about the intersection of multimedia and social justice. Since my time at KCSI & Hackbright Academy, I've become increasingly invested in issues surrounding diversity in media and tech. My calling is to empower and amplify the voices of underrepresented communities through visual storytelling.

With a background in Film & Media Studies at UC Berkeley, I'm continually perfecting my craft. Between projects, you'll usually find me at art shows, music events, festivals & exploring my beautiful hometown.
